In this section of my website I will be including my hobbies and other stuff I like to do outside of school and work. Sports

Extreme sports to regular sports are covered here

Snowboarding

The first time I ever went snowboarding was when I was 12 and since then I have been intrigued by the sport, however the second time I went was when I was 20 and now I go constantly. Learning how to snowboard was relatively easy since I have experience in other board sports, and yet it still took some time to get. Being able to freely ride down a mountain and make your own path is one of the main factors why I snowboard so much. Its a sense of freedom and fun that doesn't come with other regular sports that are usually team sports and bounded to a certain area. I ride a Salomon The Villian 155 which is also my first board. So far the mountains that I have been to range from Colorado to places like Chile, one of the best mountains I have been on is the Big 3 in Banff Canada. I don't actively seek to go down blacks since I like to do more freeriding than just bombing it down hills, having fun on the terrain you can make is where I usually will be on the mountain.


Surfing

My father has been surfing since he was in his teens and wanted to teach me how to surf, which I do enjoy surfing through my own ways as well. Being in the ocean is freeing and getting past the break of a wave and into the back of a line up is a feeling that doesnt have an exact equal to. Although I will say I am not the best at surfing I still love to watch surfing competitions and whatever videos I can find on youtube about surfing. I've surfed in England, Mexico, and California but have yet to surf in gulf coast since there are rarely any good waves down here. Whilst in California I surfed from waking up to sunset and watching the sunset as you are in the back of set felt unreal and was one of the most beautiful things I've seen. The ocean was just still and silent, as the the sunset over the shinning water. It is more than words can descirbe and how it feels. I use at the moment my longboard plus one of my father's old shortboards depending on the days we go out surfing.


Skateboarding

Whilist in highschool I got into skateboarding and stuck with it for about a year until other people stopped skating as much and I had no one to skate with, however I have recently gotten back into skateboarding and do it regularly. On the weekends I'm usaully out practing a new trick or just riding around. On campus I usually skate between my classes and around northgate. While I had a couple years of not skating I still watched major competitions like the x-games and the vans series, which kept me intrigued enough to stay into the sport and give it another try. Now I usally skate most days whether it be just to class or at the skatepark.

What I Play

At the moment the only instrument I know how to play is the guitar, but I am trying to learn Bass and Piano at some point this year. I first started learning how to play guitar when I was in middle school but after about 4 months I gave up. I tried again a year later and stayed with it for about 6 months and gave up again. The biggest factor to giving up then was that I didn't know that I had to practice as much as I had to. I instantly thought I would get better after a couple days, however I know now that isn't the case. over the course of the years I would pick up the guitar here and there and play a couple things, but never enough to really get back into it. In 2019 around April I picked it back up again and decided I would continue playing every day for a while since it was one of my resolutions for the year, and I am now coming up to a year of playing almost daily. At first I practiced about 1 hour a day and then bumped it up to about 2 hours a day, and during the summer I bumped it up to about 6 hours a day of playing. I am now coming up to a year of playing guitar and I feel like I have improved significantly, but I still have a long way to go. At the moment the guitar I use is a Fender Telecaster HH model, the amp is a line 6 spider, and the pedals I have are a ProCo Rat and a Ibanez ts9. The music I usually try to play is a bit of rock and Grunge, but I do play other genres when I feel like it.
