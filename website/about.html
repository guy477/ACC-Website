About Me

Who am I?

Early Years

I was born in Poole, England in 1999 and lived there up until I was 5-6 years old, and my family has been back almost once a year since. We moved away from England to Houston, Texas where I have been ever since. I attended elementary school, middle school, and highschool in Texas and I'm currently completeing my education at Texas A&M. I've only lived in in Poole, England and Houston, Texas. in 2014 I got my American citizenship, However I kept my English citizenship as well allowing me to become a dual citizen. In highschool I taught myself some coding and how to do 3D modeling, which made me make my final decision on which major I wanted to take up and where.


College Years

On my way to get a better education I chose Texas A&M for my college and decided to take up a major in Computer Science and get a minor in New Media Art. I am currently in my third year at Texas A&M and only switched my major once from Electrical Engineering to Computer Science. I only recently started my minor in New Media Art, which is dedicated towards digital graphics and 3D design. I'm hoping that I finish college by 2021, which would put me at graduating in 4 or 4 and a half years.
